Fund Raising




Problem:

Our project was designed to help and sustain the Badjao
Community in its struggle with financial troubles.Due to
the institution's expanding membership and the rise in
the cost of essentials, financial difficulties have been
a major problem. The institution doesn't have enough funds
to support all of its members, and they are struggling to pay for food, high-quality healthcare,
education, and other expenses like water and electricity bills. The institutions' beneficiaries
and benefactors, particularly those affected by the pandemic, struggled to find alternative sources
of income because of the lockdown, which stopped people's jobs and livelihoods. The institution
is struggling and needs more funding to fully support all of its members now that it has more than
500 members.


Project:
Our project's main objective is to assist the institution in raising money so that it can fully support each of its members. The project will increase awareness and encourage people to donate money and assist those in need. The project will target individuals of all ages with the sincere goal of assisting our Badjao friends in having a better quality of life. Since we are only organizing a fund-raising campaign project, our budget will only be 2,000 pesos. Since we will be using the internet to fund raise, we won't need money for anything other than the onsite conversation with the institution after the project is implemented. All of the money we raise through the campaign will be given to the institution and its members.

Opportunities:

With the project, the institution will have the opportunity to develop a higher standard of education and a health program for its members, particularly in light of the occurrence of viruses and disease today. In addition, the members will receive money to spend on food, better housing, clothing, and other necessities. Nevertheless, by being able to assist those in need, the project can also benefit those who contributed to the fund raising.

Rationale:
The project is highly essential since it aids in creating a secure environment where the Badjaos can access the highest standard of needs (education, healthcare, and basic needs). The project will guarantee a constant flow of supplies to meet the primary and secondary daily needs of the Badjao Community. The project will further address environmental hygiene problems that have an influence on the member's health. The administration and donation will significantly impact the community through the project's implementation because it will help to address the issues present in the community and provide a reliable source of funding for the Badjao Community.
